Tuna Sandwich

Description

What could possibly be so unnatural that God himself would not ordain such a thing to happen in nature. Only human arrogance and greed could produce a total abomination to the processes of natural selection and evolution. Yes, we are talking about the favorite treat of all cats, including Checkers--Tuna. Specifically, tuna sandwiches. Spending its entire life in the ocean until pulled from the depths by hunters with great nets, the average adult tuna weighs in at about 60 pounds. However, the largest specimens have been known to eclipse 400 pounds. Even an average tuna could eat a 10 pound orange tabby for a light afternoon snack, possibly with room for wine and a cigarette.

This is an average tuna sandwich with white bread.

Ingredients

  • White bread, no crust
  • Can 'o tuna
  • 2 springs dill
  • 1 sliced dill pickle
  • Dijon mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup Green onion
  • 1 stalk celery
  • Lettuce
  • Olive oil
    •

    Instructions

    1. Mix tuna, dill, dijon, mayonnaise, green onion, chopped celery, chopped green onion, and olive oil together.
    2. Place a bed of lettuce on the bottom have the the sandwich.
    3. Evenly spread tuna mixture on the bed of lettuce.
    4. Top your tuna mixture with sliced dill pickle
    5. If you haven't done so, please remove the crust from the sandwich and feed it to the dog.
    6. Enjoy the flavor!
